Edmonton Family Adventure
Get ready for an unforgettable trip! Edmonton offers big fun for families—explore hands-on exhibits at the TELUS World of Science, splash around at West Edmonton Mall, and unwind in scenic river valley parks.
Then, take a magical day trip to Banff, where you’ll see turquoise lakes, towering mountains, and maybe even a moose or two! It’s the perfect mix of city fun and mountain adventure for parents and kids alike.
